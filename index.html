Say goodbye to working without feedback.

Tugbat lets you see work as it happens and share it with others. Developers receive better feedback instantly. Stakeholders stay connected and make decisions with confidence.

Tugbat automatically builds previews of work, without the wait

Whether you are a developer merging code, or a content editor testing copy changes, Tugbat automatically builds previews when a change is made, and does it fast! The micro-container infrastructure in Tugbat means most of the code needed to run has already been compiled. Tugbat is ready when you need it.

Share previews with a secure link for on-the-go visual QA

Tugbat generates a secure link you can send to anyone over email, instant message…you name it. With no technical barriers, projects can enjoy immediate, non-disruptive peer review by seeing work from anywhere, on any device, with any browser.

“Tugbat has been a tremendous time-saver. Before Tugbat, our team had to stop coding to download, build, and then review each other’s work. Now we share a preview link for immediate feedback. With Tugbat, my team and I can see with what's being worked on anywhere, anytime.”

Kevin Colligan, Managing Director, Digital Media for the Grammy Awards

Hosting Agnostic

Tugbat isn’t picky about your existing workflow and hosting decisions. It is agnostic to your production environment and able to deploy your site to anywhere you want.

Customizable Previews

Tugbat's Preview API gives developers the ability to hook into the build process and take control. Common use cases include: installing QA libraries, adding test users or running testing suites.

Command Line Friendly

Every Preview comes with terminal access. Retrieve a copy of the database, run additional test, or simply get your fu on and work with your project directly.

Seamless Security

Your Tugbat account is installed on its own server, away from other Tugbat accounts. We also store your data behind a secure firewall and use an SSL/TLS tunnel to transfer files between you and us. Lastly, all Tugbat previews are accessed over an HTTPS connection for secure browsing.
